# Delrano

### Share your experience with thousands of fans and don't miss your next favorite concert.
### Relive the very best performances you've witnessed and find live music amateurs all over the world.

After quickly setting up your profile, you'll be able to review your past concerts as you wish. 
Stay tuned with upcoming events in your area and find all the information you need.

## Cloning the repository
#### Prerequisites:
Make sure to have node and npm installed before your proceed.

## Blast off!
From the root of the project, type npm start and watch the magic happen at :
localhost:5173
#### Check the API at localhost:3000/api-docs
